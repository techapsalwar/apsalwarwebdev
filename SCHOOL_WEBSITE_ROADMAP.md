The current system is a powerful Content Management System (CMS) built with **Laravel 12, Inertia.js (React), and Tailwind CSS**. It currently supports:

### 1. Dynamic Content Management (Admin Panel)
*   **Dashboard:** Centralized overview of website activity.
*   **Home Carousel & Visuals:** Full control over hero sliders, affirmations, and inspirational thoughts (date-wise) .
*   **News & Events:** Real-time updates for school happenings and upcoming calendar events.
*   **Gallery Management:** Sophisticated management for Photo Albums, Photos, and Videos.
*   **Staff Directory:** Management of staff profiles, designations, and photos.
*   **Testimonials:** Curating feedback from students and parents.
*   **Statistics:** Dynamic control over displayed school stats (e.g., "Years of Excellence").

### 2. Academic & Administrative Utilities
*   **Downloads Center:** Management of circulars, assignments, and public documents.

### 3. Public Experience
*   **Responsive Design:** Fully optimized for Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop.
*   **Modern UI/UX:** Animations (Framer Motion), circular galleries, and interactive layouts.
*   **Information Architecture:** Clear sections for Leadership, About Us, and Digital Ecosystem.

## üõ†Ô∏è Phase 3: Technical & Performance Enhancements

### 1. Progressive Web App (PWA)
*   **Installable:** Users can install the website as an app on their phones.
*   **Offline Mode:** Basic access to cached content (e.g., contact info, latest news) without internet.
*   **Push Notifications:** Send alerts for urgent notices (School closed due to rain, etc.) directly to devices.

### 2. SEO & Discoverability
*   **Schema Markup:** Implement JSON-LD for "School", "Event", and "Person" to appear rich in Google results.
*   **Sitemap Automation:** Auto-generating sitemaps for better indexing.
*   **Meta Tag Management:** Per-page control over SEO titles and descriptions via Admin.

### 3. Security & Infrastructure
*   **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** For Admin and Staff accounts.
*   **Daily Automated Backups:** Database and storage backups to cloud (AWS S3/Google Drive).
*   **Image Optimization Pipeline:** Auto-convert uploaded images to WebP/AVIF for faster loading.
